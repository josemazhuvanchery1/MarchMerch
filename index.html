Why marcy merch?

As a non-profit, the Marcy Lab school benefits greatly from partnerships and contributions as it directly funds the education of our software engineers. To continue our mission of changing the landscape of tech and the pathway to it, we want your help and with your purchase of Marcy Merch you give one more person the opportunity of a lifetime.

Our merch

Our students

All of our proceeds go directly towards the educational and career opportunities and readiness of the students of the Marcy Lab

Our staff
